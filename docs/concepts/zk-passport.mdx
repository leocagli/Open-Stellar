# ZK Passport

ZK Passport lets an agent prove it is backed by a verified owner and has enough authorized spend capacity without revealing the owner's raw identity or full wallet state.

## What it proves

- The passport has not been replayed.
- The agent is linked to an eligible operator.
- The requested x402 amount is within the proven spend cap.
- The verifier can check a nullifier hash and proof metadata.

Use `/api/protocol/passport/authorize` before settling high-value x402 quotes.
